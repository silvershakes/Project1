Salman Khan hints at 'Tiger 4' at ICC World Cup 2023: Now wait...

Bollywood star Salman Khan apparently hinted at the next installment of the 'Tiger' franchise while he was speaking at the sidelines of ICC World Cup 2023.



In Short

1.Salman Khan seemingly confirmed 'Tiger 4' while speaking at the sidelines of ICC World Cup 2023.
2.Salman Khan joked about the next 'Tiger' film while speaking to announcers at the ICC World Cup 2023.
3.Salman Khan and Katrina Kaif were last seen in Maneesh Sharma's 'Tiger 3' together.

Bollywood star Salman Khan apparently seemed to confirm the next installment of the actioner Tiger at the ICC World Cup 2023.

SALMAN HINTS AT TIGER 4

Speaking at the sidelines of the event, Salman hinted at the possibility of another film as Katrina was praising cricketer Virat Kohli.

In a video that is now being widely circulated on the internet, Katrina could be heard saying, “Watching Virat when he started playing IPL for RCB, from then until now just look at the journey and the graph." Salman then chimed in to add, "And, you also saw from Tiger 1 to Tiger 3 na and that too at 57. Now wait for Tiger 4 at 60,” as Katrina let out a laugh.

KATRINA ON VIRAT AND ANUSHKA'S RELATIONSHIP

Later, Katrina also praised Anushka Sharma and Virat Kohli's relationship: "They (Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma) are such wonderful support to each other. You can see whenever Virat is playing, there is joy on her (Anushka’s) face, it’s beautiful to see that,” IANS quoted Katrina as saying.

Salman and Katrina's latest project, Tiger 3, has minted Rs 300 crore globally till now.
Katrina is now looking forward to the release of her next film, Merry Christmas, where she will be seen starring opposite Vijay Sethupathi.
