The Daily Brief Group wins big at NT Awards 2022

The India Today Group's flagship India Today TV and Aaj Tak have once again swept the top honours at the prestigious News Television Awards 2022. Winning over 30 awards across various categories, the India Today Group emerged as the undisputed network of most-awarded channels.
India Todays Newstrack and Aaj Taks Rajtilak-Furfure Sharif won the Best News Debate Show awards in English and Hindi. India Todays To the Point won the Best Talk Show (English) while News Today took home the Best Daily News Bulletin award.
The Best TV News Presenter (male) was awarded to India Today's Rahul Kanwal, while the Best TV News Presenter (female) was taken by Aaj Tak's Sweta Singh. Ashutosh Mishra bagged the Best TV News Reporter (Male) in English.
