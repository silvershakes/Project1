A to Z: Gen-Z’s ultimate guide to understanding spirituality

Here is a common feeling that young people, Gen-Z in particular, are moving away from spiritual and religious beliefs. But it’s just a misconception as many youngsters have embraced a new-age of spirituality with its own fascinating lexicon.


There is a common feeling that young people, Gen-Z in particular, are moving away from spiritual and religious beliefs. But it’s just a misconception as many youngsters have embraced a new-age of spirituality with its own fascinating lexicon.

So, get ready to learn your ABCs of spirituality Gen-Z style

A- Affirmations : Affirmations are like those daily hype-up messages you send yourself. It’s all about positive self-talk, where you repeat cool stuff to boost your confidence. You can look in the mirror and say, “You go, girl!”, or just jot it down in your journal.

B- Bhakti : Bhakti is like fangirling on something way more cosmic than Taylor Swift. It’s all about having a crazy amount of love and devotion for a higher power or a spiritual idea. It involves spreading love and good energy like your favourite influencers do on social media.

C- Chakra :Chakras are like your body’s energy centres. Think of them as your power-up points in a video game. Each chakra is like a different level, and when you unlock them all, you’re basically an Avenger with all the right moves.

D- Dharma :Dharma is like your cosmic rulebook for living your best life. It’s all about being a decent human being and doing your part in the universe. Basically, it’s the ancient way of saying, “Just be a good person, dude!”

E- Enlightenment : Enlightenment is like leveling up in the game of life until you reach the ultimate stage of awareness and inner peace. Think of it as becoming the James Bond of your mind, equipped with the gadgets of deep understanding and a license to chill.

F- Feng Shui : Feng Shui is like the OG way to set up your space for those good vibes. It’s all about creating a home or office that flows with energy like a perfectly choreographed Tiktok dance routine. Think of it as the art of making your crib the coolest hangout spot in the metaverse.

G- Gratitude journal : Imagine you’ve got this super cool diary, but instead of writing about your daily hustle, you’re jotting down things you’re thankful for, from that amazing latte you had this morning to your bestie’s hilarious memes. This journal reminds you that even on the crummiest days, there’s a ton of stuff to be grateful for.

H- Healing Crystals : From amethyst (the chill pill) to rose quartz (the love magnet), these fancy rocks are believed to have cosmic powers. Just pick the one that vibes with your mood and let the good juju flow.

I- Incense sticks : Incense sticks are like your mystical room fresheners. The fragrant swirls of smoke connect you with the spiritual world. Whether you’re meditating, doing yoga, or just chilling, incense sets the mood and clears the cosmic clutter.

J- JOMO : JOMO is the cool cousin of FOMO. It stands for ‘Joy of Missing Out.’ JOMO is like the ultimate “me time” when you’re perfectly content with not being in the mix.

K- Karma : Karma is like the universe’s boomerang. You know, when you throw good vibes out there, they come back to you like a cosmic high-five. But if you’re spreading negativity, well, watch out for the cosmic facepalm. It’s like the ultimate cause and effect in life, where what goes around, comes around.

L- Lucid dreaming : It’s like being inside the movie Inception where you’re able to create your own universe, but only this time you can control the plot, fly like a superhero, or even have a chat with your dream characters, all at your own will.
