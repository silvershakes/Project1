HTET 2023 admit card released | Steps to download

The Board of School Education, Haryana, has released the HTET 2023 admit card. Candidates can check the details here.



The Board of School Education, Haryana, has released the admit cards for the upcoming Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test (HTET) 2023 examination. Candidates who have registered for HTET 2023 can visit the official website of the state board, i.e., bseh.org.in, to check and download their respective admit cards. The board released the admit cards on November 24.

As per the information, the board has scheduled the HTET 2023 examination for December 2 and 3. The Level 3 examination will be held on December 2, from 3 pm to 5:30 pm. The Level 2 examination will be held on December 3, 2023, from 10 am to 12:30 pm and the Level 1 examination will be held on December 3 from 3 pm to 5:30 pm.

The registration process for HTET 2023 concluded on November 11, 2023.

*HOW TO DOWNLOAD*

To download the HTET 2023 admit cards, candidates can follow these steps:
Step 1: Visit the official website, i.e., bseh.org.in.
Step 2: On the homepage, click the 'HTET 2023 admit card' link.
Step 3: A new page will appear on the screen.
Step 4: Enter the asked credentials and click on submit.
Step 5: Your admit card will appear on the screen.
Step 6: Download your admit card and take a printout of it for future reference.

Candidates can click here for the direct link to download the HTET 2023 admit card. For more details, candidates are requested to visit the official website of the board.

The HTET 2023 examination will be conducted on December 2 and 3, 2023. On December 2 the exam will be held from 3 pm to 5: 30 pm and on December 3 the examination will be held from 10 am to 12:30 pm and from 3 pm to 5:30 pm.
