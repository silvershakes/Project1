'Arjan Vailly' out: Ranbir's 'Animal' song promises goosebumps-inducing scenes

'Arjan Vailly' is the latest musical release from Ranbir Kapoor's upcoming film 'Animal'. The makers released the song on social media today, November 18.



Ranbir Kapoor's highly-anticipated upcoming film, 'Animal', will hit theatres on December 1. Directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga, it has been receiving a great response from the audience. After 'Hua Main', 'Satranga' and 'Papa Meri Jaan', a new song titled 'Arjan Vailly' has from 'Animal' has been released by the makers. It features Ranbir Kapoor in intense fight scenes.

'ANIMAL' SONG ARJAN VAILLY OUT

Animal', directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga, features Ranbir Kapoor, Bobby Deol, Rashmika Mandanna and Triptii Dimrii in key roles. The story of the movie explores the father-son relationship between Ranbir and Anil's characters.

'Animal’s' latest musical release 'Arjan Vailly' has commanding vocals and lyrics by Bhupinder Babbal and composition by Manan Bhardwaj. The track shows the character development of Ranbir Kapoor. He is seen in a bloody avatar as he goes on a killing spree at a hotel lobby. Ranbir is seen fighting off goons sporting animal masks. The lyrics are quite catchy.

After 'Kabir Singh', 'Animal' is Sandeep Reddy Vanga's second Bollywood film. It is slated for a theatrical release on December 1, and will clash with Vicky Kaushal's 'Sam Bahadur.' The film was earlier scheduled to release in August, but due to a clash with Sunny Deol's 'Gadar 2', Akshay Kumar's 'OMG 2' and Rajinikanth's 'Jailer', the director postponed the date.

Anil Kapoor plays the role of Ranbir Kapoor's father Balbir Singh in 'Animal'. Rashmika Mandanna will be seen as Geetanjali, Ranbir's love interest. Bobby Deol will play the role of antagonist.
